Alt-B: move backward one word
Ctrl-U: delete all before cursor
Ctrl-K: delete all after cursor
Ctrl-W: delete previous word
Ctrl-V: paste
!!: last command
less +F (instead of tail)
Ctrl-x-e: continue editing command in editor
Alt-.: paste last word of previous command
reset: unbork terminal
